Orders & Payment

There are several ways to check the status of your order:

  1. Account Dashboard: Log in to your account and go to the "Order History" section to view all your orders and their current status.
  2. Order Confirmation Email: Use the tracking link provided in your order confirmation email.
  3. Order Tracking Page: Visit our "Track Order" page and enter your order number and email address.
  4. Customer Support: Contact our customer service team with your order number for assistance.

We provide regular updates on your order status, from processing to shipping and delivery.

We accept a variety of payment methods to accommodate our customers' preferences:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • Shop Pay
  • Bank transfers (for select countries)

All payment information is securely processed and encrypted to ensure your data remains safe. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers.

Order modifications and cancellations are possible, but time-sensitive:

  • Orders can be modified or canceled within 1 hour of placement.
  • Once an order enters the processing stage, modifications are limited and cancellations may not be possible.
  • If your order has been processed but not yet shipped, we may be able to accommodate changes on a case-by-case basis.
  • Once shipped, orders cannot be canceled, but you can return the item after delivery according to our return policy.

To request modifications or cancellation, contact our customer service team immediately with your order number.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy for most items. To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • In the same condition that you received it
  • Unused and unwashed
  • In the original packaging with all tags attached
  • Returned within 30 days of the delivery date

Please note that certain products are non-returnable for hygiene reasons, including earphones, underwear, and personalized items. Digital products and gift cards are also non-returnable.

To initiate a return, log into your account, locate your order, and select "Return Items." Follow the instructions to print a return label and choose your preferred refund method.

Our refund process typically follows this timeline:

  1. Once we receive your return, we'll inspect the item(s) within 1-2 business days.
  2. Upon approval, we'll process your refund to the original payment method within 3 business days.
  3. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment provider:
    • Credit/debit cards: 5-10 business days
    • PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay: 3-5 business days
    • Store credit: Immediate

You'll receive email notifications at each step of the refund process. If you haven't received your refund after the expected timeframe, please contact our customer service team.

Technical Questions

Our 3D product viewer is designed to work on modern browsers and devices. For the best experience, we recommend:

Desktop Browsers:

  • Chrome (version 75+)
  • Firefox (version 67+)
  • Safari (version 12.1+)
  • Edge (Chromium-based, version 79+)

Mobile Browsers:

  • iOS Safari (iOS 12+)
  • Android Chrome (version 75+)

Your device should have WebGL enabled and sufficient graphics capabilities. Most modern smartphones and computers from the last 4-5 years should work well. If you experience performance issues, try reducing the quality setting in the 3D viewer options menu.

If you're experiencing issues with our 3D product viewer, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check your browser: Make sure you're using a supported browser (see above) and it's updated to the latest version.
  2. Enable WebGL: Ensure WebGL is enabled in your browser settings.
  3. Clear cache: Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, then reload the page.
  4. Disable extensions: Some browser extensions might interfere with the 3D viewer. Try disabling them temporarily.
  5. Check internet connection: A stable internet connection is needed for the 3D models to load properly.
  6. Try a different device: If possible, try accessing the 3D viewer from another device or computer.

If you've tried these steps and still experience issues, please contact our technical support team with details about your device, browser, and the specific product you're trying to view.
